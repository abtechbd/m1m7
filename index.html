Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

“Innovation survives only when people believe in their own ideas. “

Sustainability

There are three dimensions in sustainable development: environmental, social and economic.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure encompasses essential facilities, systems, and structures supporting societal, economic, and organizational

Empowerment

Empowerment is fostering confidence, autonomy, and capability to make decisions and achieve goals.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

The vision of the future is a harmonious blend of technological innovation and sustainable living. It envisions a world where artificial intelligence, renewable energy, and scientific breakthroughs coexist with environmental preservation, fostering equity, connectivity, and prosperity. This future prioritizes human well-being, inclusivity, and collective progress for generations to come.
